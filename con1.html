Individual Twitter Account Analysis (ABC News)

What We Found

As the graph shows, the tweets from ABC news are most likely to be in the crime, politics, sports and entertainment categories. It can be deduced from this that if you only follow the ABC news Twitter account, you will most likely receive news from these categories.

In order to broaden your focus and get a broader view of the news, we suggest that you follow accounts related to the travel, green and business to ensure that your news sources are comprehensive.

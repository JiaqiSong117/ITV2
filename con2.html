Sky News

What We Found


Once you have entered the Sky News users name (SkyNews) and clicked on the search button, a bar chart like this will be generated.

As the graph shows, the tweets from Sky news are most likely to be in the entertainment, crime and sport categories. It can be deduced from this that if you follow the Sky news Twitter account, you will most likely receive news from these categories.

In order to broaden your focus and get a broader view of the news, we suggest that you follow accounts related to the travel , green and business to ensure that your news sources are comprehensive.
